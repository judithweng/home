Stitch Length

Stitch length determines how long each stitch is. With a shorter length, less fabric is pulled. With a longer length, more fabric is pulled. The numbers in the dial represent the length in milimeters (mm)

The standard length is usually 2.5mm, but you should adjust this depending on the type of frabric you are working with. Heavier and thicker materials require a longer stitch.
