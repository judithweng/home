Bobbin Winder

The bobbin winder is a spindle on the top of your sewing machine. Then, step on the foot pedal for your bobbin to start winding. The machine will automatically stop winding once the bobbin is full.

This function is activated differently in each machine. For Singer 2273, you just push the bobbin winder to the right. Other machines, however, require you to pull out the hand wheel.
