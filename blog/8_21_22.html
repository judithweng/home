Welcome Blog!

Ever since I started to put together this website, I always thought of adding a 'blog' component to it. I have been hesitating all this time because I thought that no one would read it and no one really cares about what a 21-year old college girl is going through. These may be true but I decided that I really wanted to document my growth and my thoughts throughout my journey.
I do keep a journal where I handwrite entries in Spanish once in a while. This year, however, I have been traveling to different states which has made it hard to carry items around me, which is why I decided on this blog! I hope I can write to you every week or every other week if my schedule gets a bit hectic.

Song of the week: Home by Janet Suhh
