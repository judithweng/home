Half Way

Almost a month has passed since I last wrote here. There is indeed a lot going on. I have talked to several professors and counselors who have advised me and encouraged me to apply to a PhD program. The past few weeks I have been working on my application while at the same time managing school work. This is perhaps one of the most fun semesters I have had so far. Philosophy has been extremely life changing and has made me become more aware of my surroundings. Art has made me look at objects for an extremely long time, which is tiring, but I have developed the ability to notice the details of an object and their color value. My engineering classes involve a lot of coding but they lead to material results that you can see and feel. I have also somewhat chosen my senior thesis. I still cannot believe I am a senior!

Song of the week: This City (with Camilo)

"It scares me when I stop dead and see how fast time has passed"

It is already fall break. A mark that shows we are half way through the semester. There is a lot that I want to do with my friends and classmates. It scares me that we might not see each other again once we graduate, which is why I just want to stay right here and right now forever.
