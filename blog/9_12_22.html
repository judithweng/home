Finding a path

Entering September has made me think a lot of how the year has just been moving forward and how can I move forward with it? Sometimes I feel like I am stationary and not doing much. I do not see much when I think of my future. I want to continue learning so perhaps grad school but I also want to help my family financially so perhaps workforce. Time really is flying but I just want to stop and take a break.

Song of the week: Feel Good by Gryffin

"I doubt myself and I'm too proud to ask for help."

I have no idea what I am doing that I get scared to show that. I do not want to ask questions nor for help. What if doing that will make me feel inferior? What if they think I am dumb?
