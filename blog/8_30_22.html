Last First Day

A few days late to this journal but I just started my last fall semester as an undergraduate! It feels weird. I still feel like a freshman! I have not been on campus for 9 months and I am mostly seeing unfamiliar faces. I am excited for all of the classes I will be taking this semester because I have finished all of my prereqs and can finally take any classes I want. Hope I keep this excitement all semester long! *fingers crossed*

Song of the week: Me Fui de Vacaciones by Bad Bunny

"A shot for the good friendships and for all the blessings"

This summer was, without a doubt, the best summer of my life. I got to try so many new things and stepped out of my comfort zone. I specifically became very social to the point some people could not believe I was shy. This is for me, one of my biggest accomplishments. I will forever cherish every individual I met and all of the memories I created with them.
