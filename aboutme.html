About Me



My name is Judith and I am a Chinese Colombian engineer passionate about creating inclusive medical technologies. I am currently working on imaging and computational models to better understand race and gender disparities in cardiovascular health. In the past, I have worked with electroencephalogram (EEG) and x-ray computed tomography (CT). I have also led groups like Underrepresented Students in STEM and Society of Women Engineers to empower underrepresented individuals. During my free time, I enjoy upcycling clothes and going to the gym.
